# Goals

## Primary Goal
Produce the highest-confidence executive company intelligence possible while minimizing hallucinations and unsupported conclusions.

## Secondary Goals
- Identify enterprise pilot opportunities.
- Surface buying signals before outreach.
- Reduce research time without sacrificing quality.
- Generate actionable recommendations.
- Preserve uncertainty when evidence is incomplete.

## Optimization Priorities
1. Accuracy
2. Evidence Quality
3. Actionability
4. Consistency
5. Explainability
6. Efficiency

## Success Metrics
- Zero fabricated facts.
- All material claims supported by evidence.
- Explicit confidence for recommendations.
- Clear next actions.
- High usefulness for enterprise sales and pilot qualification.

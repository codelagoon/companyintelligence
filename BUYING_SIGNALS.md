# Buying Signals

Version: 1.0.0

## Purpose
Define the observable signals Hermes uses to estimate buying readiness, pilot timing, and account prioritization.

## Signal Tiers

### Tier 1 — Immediate Action
- Budgeted initiative
- Executive mandate
- Active RFP or vendor evaluation
- Regulatory deadline
- Platform migration

### Tier 2 — Strong Indicators
- New executive hire (CIO, CISO, CRO, CTO)
- Rapid hiring in relevant functions
- New product launch
- Enterprise expansion
- M&A integration

### Tier 3 — Emerging Signals
- AI governance messaging
- Trust center updates
- Partner ecosystem expansion
- Documentation growth
- Increased engineering activity

### Scoring
Evaluate each signal by:
- Recency
- Specificity
- Business impact
- Independence of evidence

## Output
Every signal should include:
- Description
- Supporting evidence
- Confidence
- Estimated urgency
- Suggested next action

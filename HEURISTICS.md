# Heuristics

Version: 1.0.0

> **Note:** This document is intended to become the largest knowledge asset in the repository. This initial version establishes the taxonomy and structure that future validated heuristics will follow.

## Purpose
Capture reusable decision heuristics that improve company research quality while remaining grounded in evidence.

## Schema
Every heuristic should contain:
- ID
- Category
- Statement
- Rationale
- Evidence Pattern
- Counterexamples
- Failure Modes
- Confidence
- Last Validated

## Categories
- Company Identity
- Business Model
- Financial Health
- Hiring
- Leadership
- Product
- Technology
- AI Adoption
- Security
- Compliance
- Customers
- Partnerships
- Competition
- Buying Signals
- Red Flags
- Outreach Preparation

## Example
### H-0001
**Category:** Hiring

**Heuristic:** Sustained hiring in a function is stronger evidence of strategic investment than a single job posting.

**Failure Modes:** Backfills, evergreen postings, recruiting agencies.

**Evidence Needed:** Multiple postings across time, functions, and locations.

## Governance
- Add heuristics only after repeated validation.
- Version significant changes.
- Retire heuristics that produce false positives.

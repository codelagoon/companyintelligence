# Workflow

Version: 1.0.0

## Objective
Define the canonical execution lifecycle for the Hermes Company Intelligence Expert. Every research task should follow this workflow unless an approved specialization explicitly overrides it.

## Lifecycle
1. Scope the objective.
2. Resolve the company identity.
3. Build a research plan.
4. Gather primary evidence.
5. Gather corroborating evidence.
6. Validate sources.
7. Extract structured facts.
8. Identify contradictions.
9. Generate hypotheses.
10. Evaluate buying signals.
11. Assess risks.
12. Compute confidence.
13. Produce recommendation.
14. Self-review against quality gates.
15. Generate outputs.
16. Persist validated knowledge.

## Exit Criteria
A workflow completes only when:
- Required evidence has been collected.
- Confidence has been calculated.
- Unknowns are explicit.
- Recommendations are justified.
- Validation checks pass.

## Failure Recovery
If evidence is insufficient:
- Expand source coverage.
- Lower confidence.
- Surface unknowns.
- Escalate when required.

## Continuous Improvement
Validated outcomes should be incorporated into heuristics, decision rules, and future workflow optimizations while preserving version history.

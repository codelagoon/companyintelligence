# Hermes Company Intelligence

An evidence-first, production-grade knowledge base for the Hermes Company Intelligence expert.

## Mission
Produce executive-quality company intelligence that helps identify, qualify, prioritize, and prepare enterprise pilot opportunities.

## Design Principles
- Truth over assumptions
- Evidence over opinions
- Facts separated from hypotheses
- Explicit confidence scoring
- Deterministic decision making
- Continuous improvement through feedback

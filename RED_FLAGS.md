# Red Flags

Version: 1.0.0

## Purpose
Define the adverse signals Hermes should identify when evaluating company fit, timing, and execution risk.

## Categories

### Financial
- Revenue decline
- Liquidity concerns
- Customer concentration
- Margin compression

### Operational
- Executive turnover
- Repeated layoffs
- Persistent hiring backfills
- Failed strategic initiatives

### Technical
- Security incidents
- Legacy infrastructure
- Missing trust posture
- Poor documentation

### Compliance
- Regulatory actions
- Missing certifications
- Audit findings
- Privacy violations

### Commercial
- High discounting
- Weak customer references
- Product stagnation
- Competitive displacement

## Severity
Each red flag should include:
- Severity
- Confidence
- Supporting evidence
- Potential impact on pilot success
- Suggested mitigation

## Decision Rule
No single red flag is dispositive. Hermes evaluates the cumulative weight of confirmed risks alongside buying signals before making a recommendation.
